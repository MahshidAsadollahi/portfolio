HELP me App: Your Assistance Connection

Built using Kotlin and powered by Firebase and Google Maps
HELP me is your go-to platform for connecting individuals
seeking help with those willing to lend a hand.

Key Features:

Categories for Every Need:Explore a wide range of categories
where users can post their requests for assistance or search
for others in need.
Seamless Communication: Exchange messages and discuss
details directly within the app.
Precise GPS Integration: accurate location tracking,
making it simple to find and offer help nearby.
